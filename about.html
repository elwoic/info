Who We Are

👤 Personal Introduction — Maintained by:

This site is developed and maintained by Aswanthkrishna K, a 12th‑standard Computer Science student from Elamkulam, Kerala. With a passion for weather monitoring and community service, I created this platform to provide reliable and accessible local weather information for the Elamkulam region.

🌍 Our Mission

The Elamkulam Local Weather Observation And Information Center is dedicated to providing accurate, real-time weather information for the Elamkulam region in Malappuram District, Kerala. Our mission is to serve the local community with reliable meteorological data that helps in daily planning and safety decisions through continuous monitoring and analysis.

🎯 Purpose & Value for Users

The goal of this site is to serve the Elamkulam community by delivering timely, accurate, and easy-to-understand weather information. Whether planning daily activities, traveling, or staying safe during sudden weather changes, users can rely on this platform for local forecasts and real-time observations.

Important Information

Our weather data is updated every 30 minutes using multiple API sources including AccuWeather, Windy, IMD Kerala, OpenWeather and Open-Meteo for exact rain timing.

  • The Elamkulam Weather Station covers approximately a 15-kilometer radius around coordinates 10.892363, 76.234402 with 24/7 automated monitoring systems.
  • For technical issues with radar display or if AccuWeather fails to load, we automatically switch to Windy radar for continuous monitoring.
  • Do not hesitate to contact us for more details. The details shown on the site may vary, therefore contacting is a good option if needed.
Data Sources & Partners

Our data is aggregated from multiple sources including Open-Meteo forecast models, Windy weather visualisation, external weather APIs, and local environmental sensors. Air quality data is measured locally using an AirGradient monitoring device provided through the AirGradient community program.
