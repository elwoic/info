1. Introduction

The Elamkulam Local Weather Observation & Information Centre (ELWOIC) is an independent, non-profit community initiative dedicated to providing real-time, reliable, and accessible weather information for the Elamkulam region of Kerala, India. Established to improve local environmental awareness, we deliver simplified weather data to help communities make informed daily decisions with a focus on transparency and public benefit.

2. Background & Establishment

Founded by Aswanth Krishna K, ELWOIC was born from the need for location-specific data. Regional forecasts often miss micro-level variations in specific localities. ELWOIC bridges this gap with community-focused insights, maintained independently for reliability and public service.

3. Mission

To serve the Elamkulam community with timely, accurate, and clearly presented weather information to enhance awareness, safety, and preparedness.

4. Vision

To expand into a wider network of community-supported observation points across Kerala, improving grassroots environmental understanding.

5. Core Functions & Services

Real-Time Monitoring Continuous observation of local atmospheric conditions.
Weather Forecasting Presentation of forecast data relevant to our local region.
Interactive Dashboard A centralized platform for current and past weather info.
Historical Data Access to recorded data for reference and analysis.
Community Reports A system for residents to share real-time observations.
Data Request System Facility for personal or research-specific data requests.
Public Awareness Dissemination of insights to improve understanding.

6. Data and Observational Scope

Our data is derived from ground-based observations, radar, and satellite references, focusing on:

Temperature
Humidity
Rainfall
Wind Speed & Direction
Atmospheric Pressure
Sky Conditions

7. Importance & Community Relevance

Kerala's dynamic weather patterns require hyperlocal insights. ELWOIC provides value for:

  • Daily planning and commuting
  • Agricultural activities
  • Students & Educational institutions
  • Outdoor workers & small businesses
  • Public safety during adverse weather

8. Community Participation

We are a collaborative initiative. Strengthening the system involves: submission of local observations, providing feedback/suggestions, and engaging with shared data updates.
